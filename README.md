# 🛒 Blinkit Sales Performance Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes Blinkit sales data to evaluate revenue performance, customer purchasing behavior, and product category trends. The dashboard supports business decision-making by identifying growth opportunities and operational improvements.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective
To analyze sales performance and optimize revenue by:
- Tracking total sales & revenue trends
- Identifying top-performing product categories
- Understanding customer purchase patterns
- Improving inventory and pricing strategy

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies Used
- Power BI (Dashboard Development)
- Excel (Data Cleaning & Transformation)
- SQL (SSMS – Data Aggregation & KPI Extraction)
- DAX (Calculated Metrics & Measures)

---

## 📂 Dataset Description
The dataset includes:
- Product categories
- Sales amount
- Outlet size & location
- Item type & visibility
- Customer ratings
- Transaction details

---

## 📊 Key KPIs Developed
- Total Revenue
- Average Order Value
- Sales by Product Category
- Sales by Outlet Type
- Revenue Trend Over Time
- Top 10 Performing Products

---

## 🔍 Key Insights
- Tier-1 outlets generated higher overall revenue.
- Certain product categories consistently outperformed others.
- Medium-sized outlets showed better sales efficiency.
- Higher product visibility correlated with increased sales.

---

## 📈 Business Impact
This dashboard helps business stakeholders to:
- Identify revenue-driving product categories
- Optimize inventory allocation
- Improve outlet performance monitoring
- Support strategic expansion planning
